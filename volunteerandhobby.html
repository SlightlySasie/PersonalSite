Volunteer and Hobby Projects

Lastly a big part of this gallery consists of playing cards for a pirate card game called Skull King. The game was discontinued and we ruined our card set by playing too much, so I took the liberty to redesign the entire deck myself. I created the playing deck in GIMP and edited pictures of my friends (and their dogs) as the pirates.
