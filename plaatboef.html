Website Redesign: De Plaatboef

I started this project as a way to practice designing in Figma. The website of De Plaatboef was chosen because, in my opinion, their current website is quite outdated, both in design and available information. The site can be seen on the "Inspiration and Assets Board". I found this design choice a shame, as the business has a very interesting style and customer base, which should be reflected in their website.

The main goal of this practice was to redesign a website in a short time frame. I kept the original logo and background of De Plaatboef website intact and incorporated them in a more interesting and different way compared to how it was done on the original website. The secondary goal of this exercise was designing a coherent way to disclose all the information of the business, which are currently not found on the website. This information includes both of the locations, the multiple ways to find their stores and products on social media and the various ways to contact the business.
