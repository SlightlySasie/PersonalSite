Problem Space

Our desk research shows that high school graduates often struggle to find an university and study that fullfils all their wishes. This is especially true for students who consider studying abroad, as this greatly increases the possibilities. Addressing these challenges in with a recommendation application that gathers the needs and wants of a student in a fun and engaging way can help the young adults form better informed opinions on their next steps.
